Cheese and Jam Sandwich Recipe!!!

  1. Put in your working station a plate, two slices of bread, spreading knife, jam, and a single packaged piece of cheese (of your choice).
  2. Put the two pieces of bread on top of your plate.
  3. Open your bottle of jam and use the spreading knife to take out some of it out of the bottle.
  4. Use the jam that is now on your spreading knife and spread it on top of the two slices of bread. Feel free to add as much jam as desired.
  5. Open up your single packed piece of cheese, discard the wrapping and place the cheese on top of one of the slices of bread on your plate.
  6. Put together both slices and enjoy your sandwich.
